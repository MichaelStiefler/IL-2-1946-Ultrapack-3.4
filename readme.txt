Ultrapack 3.4 Cassie

Installation:

Download all parts of the update (the number of parts depends on the Option you choose, Option 1 - the smallest - hast 9 parts).
Run the installer ("Ultrapack 3.4 Cassie <Option Description>.part1.exe") and let the installer do it's job.
